Meat Double Sushi Roll

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Plastic wrap
  • Vegetables
  • Egg
  • Ground beef
  • Roasted seaweed
  • Pork sung

Instructions

  1. First of all, make sure your rice at least an hour or two in advance so that it has time to cool to room temperature before assembling your rolls.
  2. Then, add the seasonings, eggs to the beef ground and mix well. spread a thin layer of beef ground over the nori . Put in the oven and bake for 20-30 mins.
  3. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori and add the fillings, add your ingredients toward the center of the rice-covered nori. Don’t forget roll your beef sushi in and don't overpack it.
  4. Roll up the sushi.

Healthy Whole Wheat Waffles

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Coconut flour
  • Low fat milk
  • Rice cake
  • Yeast/Sweetener
  • Waffle maker

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl whisk together the whole wheat flour, coconut flour, low-fat milk, sweetener, yeast and pour some water until well combined.
  2. Then, start to roll it into a ball.
  3. Cover the dough with plastic warp and waiting for 50mins.
  4. Tuck the rice cake into the dough
  5. Preheat your waffle maker. Cook the waffles until golden brown and crisp, wait for 1-2mins.

Deep Fried Prawns

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Green onion
  • Soy source / sugar / oyster sauce /
  • Oil / Salt
  • Frozen Green bean

Instructions

  1. Soak the shrimp in warm, lightly salted water for 5 minutes.
  2. Prepare all the ingredients. Mix all the seasoning well.
  3. Rinse in cold water, drain and pat dry with paper towels. Cut off the head and whisks.
  4. Heat the oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Put the garlic and ginger into the pot. Then place the prawns in the hot oil and turning as needed, until golden brown.
  5. Put all the seasonings in and sprinkle the green beans and chopped green onion on top.
